Beetle Chocolates

Just an idea I had since Coraline's "other" mother eats beetles. Theming!

Ingredients

  • A lot, Meltable baking chocolate or candy melts.
  • Brazil nuts
  • Mini Pretzels

Steps

  1. Melt the chocolate
  2. Dip Brazil nuts in chocolate
  3. Break off even bits of the pretzel
  4. Rest pretzel bits over the brazil nut, like legs
  5. Freeze to set?
